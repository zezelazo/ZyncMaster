How it works

Three steps, then it runs itself.

For people who are tired of keeping things in sync across their devices — PCs, Macs and phones — by hand, over and over. No scripts, no exports, no copy-paste: connect once and Zync Master keeps your calendar mirrored on a schedule you control.

1

Read your source

Read your local Outlook through the app, or sign in to an online account. Zync only ever sees the calendars you pick.

2

Mirror the next 14 days

Choose what mirrors where. The source stays read-only — events flow one way to your destination calendar.

3

Let it sync, quietly

It runs on a timer in the tray and shows every change as it happens. Step away — both calendars stay in lockstep.

Your data never leaves your control.

Zync Master runs on your machines and your own cloud. There's no third-party server sitting between you and your data.

Local-first by design

Sync runs directly between your devices and your providers. Nothing is resold or mined.

Tokens stay encrypted

You authorise through Microsoft directly. Zync holds a revocable, encrypted token — never your password.

The past is never touched

Only events inside your chosen window are mirrored. History on either side stays exactly as you left it.
